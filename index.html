What is a Photon

A photon is a particle representing a quantum of light or other electromagnetic radiation. It has zero rest mass and travels at the speed of light.

Solar Energy Conversion

Photovoltaic cells convert photons into electrical energy by absorbing light and generating free electrons through the photoelectric effect.

Photovoltaic cells harness solar photons to produce electricity.
